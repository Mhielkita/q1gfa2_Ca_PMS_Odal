TOMICA: A Premium Quality Die-Cast Toy Car Line


Tomica is a line of die-cast toy vehicles produced by the Japanese toy company Tomy. The brand includes a wide range of vehicles, including cars, trucks, buses, and emergency vehicles, and is notable for its high level of detail and accuracy in replicating real-life vehicles. Tomica vehicles are popular among both children and adults, and collectors often seek out rare or vintage pieces to add to their collections. The brand has also inspired a number of spin-off products, including video games and animated series.


Toyota Crown Comfort Taxi Tomica


The Tomica Crown Comfort Taxi is a die-cast vehicle made by Tomy, featuring precise details of the real Toyota Crown Comfort taxi in Japan. Collector's love its interior, exterior, and traditional yellow and black paint with taxi marks. The model is durable and perfect for display or play.
